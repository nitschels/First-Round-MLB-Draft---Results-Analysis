# First-Round-MLB-Draft---Results-Analysis
A results-based analysis looking at the success of the last 25 years of the first round of the MLB Draft. Analyzing the success of advances in MLB scouting

Using MLB draft data collected from Baseball Reference, I will look at the past 25 years of the first round of the MLB draft and how successful each draft was based on 'flop rate,' a statistic I define in my report, along with WAR stats, comparing position players, draft level, and other factors.
